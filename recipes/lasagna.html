Homemade Lasagna

Description

A tasty homemade lasagna ready for any dinner occasion. The succulent flavors hidden within this dish will have your guests salviating with anticipation of their first bite.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
